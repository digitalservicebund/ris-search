Act on Friends and Goldfishs

(Freunde und Goldfische Gesetz – FG)

Translation provided by the Federal Ministry of Something

Version information: The translation includes the amendment(s) to the Act by Article 1 of the Act of 03 March 2012.

Section 1
Purpose of the Act

(1) This Act aims to regulate the social relationships between friends and the welfare of pet goldfishs. It establishes basic principles of friendship and guidelines for the responsible ownership of goldfishs, recognizing their unique symbiotic relationship with humans.

(2) All provisions of this Act apply to natural persons and their pet goldfishs residing within the territorial scope of this Act.

Section 2
Obligations of friendship

(1) A friend is obliged to provide emotional support to another friend in times of need. This includes, but is not limited to, listening to problems and providing comfort.

(2) A friend may not, without prior written consent, borrow personal property from another friend for a period longer than 72 hours. Exceptions are permitted in cases of emergency.

Section 3
Goldfish welfare

(1) Every goldfish owner must provide a tank of at least 20 litres per fish. The tank must be equipped with a functional filter and sufficient aeration to maintain water quality.

(2) Goldfishs must be fed at least once per day with food appropriate for their species. Owners are forbidden from feeding goldfishs human food, with the exception of peas.

Section 4
Regulatory offences

(1) Regulatory offences are committed by parties that wilfully or negligently:

1. Fail to provide emotional support in contravention of Section 2 (1).

2. Borrow personal property in contravention of Section 2 (2).

3. Fail to provide the minimum tank size for a goldfish.

4. Feed a goldfish inappropriate food.

(2) Regulatory offences are punishable by a fine of up to 100 euros for friendship-related offences and up to 500 euros for goldfish-related offences.
